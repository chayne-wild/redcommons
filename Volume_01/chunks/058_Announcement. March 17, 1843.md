# Announcement. March 17, 1843 

ANNOUNCEMENT147

The undersigned declares that, owing to the present conditions of censorship, he has retired as from today from the editorial board of the Rheinische Zeitung.

Cologne, March 17, 1843

Dr. Marx

[^1]: First published in the Rheinische Zeitung No. 77, March 18, 1843

[^2]: Printed according to the newspaper Published in English for the first time
