POSTSCRIPT BY HEINRICH MARX

TO HENRIETTE MARX'S LETTER

TO KARL MARX IN BERLIN207

[February 15-16, 1838] Dear Karl,

I send you a few words of greeting, I cannot do much yet.

Your father

Marx
