# 24. To Marie Engels, September 28 



**[p. 469]**

I enclose masses of poems. Share them between you.

Yours,

24

TO MARIE ENGELS

Bremen, Sept. 28, 1839

Dear Marie,

It was high time your ladyship wrote to me at last; it has gone on long enough, Mamsell. But I'll forgive you your great crimes and tell you something. Tomorrow it will be two weeks since we rode out to Delmenhorst. This is a small Oldenburg country town
