# Marx's Undertaking Not to Publish Anything in Belgium on Current Politics. March 22, 1845 



**[p. 677]**

4

MARX'S UNDERTAKING NOT TO PUBLISH ANYTHING IN BELGIUM ON CURRENT POLITICS251

To obtain permission to reside in Belgium I agree to pledge myself, on my word of honour, not to publish in Belgium any work on current politics.

March 22, 1845 Dr. Karl Marx

First published in L'Europe Nouvelle Printed according to the manu- No. 346, 1924 script Translated from the French
