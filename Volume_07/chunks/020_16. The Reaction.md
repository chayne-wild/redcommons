# 16. The Reaction 



**[p. 45]**

THE REACTION

Cologne, June 5. The dead ride fast.3 Herr Camphausen disavows the revolution and the reaction dares to suggest to the Agreement Assembly that the revolution should be stigmatised as a riot. On June 3, a deputy6 moved that a monument be erected for the soldiers who died on March 18.

[^1]: Written on June 5, 1848 Printed according to the newspaper

[^2]: First published in the Neue Rheinische Published in English for the first Zeitung No. 6, June 6, 1848 time
