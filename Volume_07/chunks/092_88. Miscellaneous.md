# 88. Miscellaneous 



**[p. 314]**

MISCELLANEOUS2

Very shortly a Bill on defamation along entirely new lines will be laid before the Chamber. Our criticism of the article of the Code Napoléon in connection with Hecker's suit against the Neue Rheinische Zeitung was evidently only too well founded.3

Written by Marx on August 2, 1848 Printed according to the newspaper
