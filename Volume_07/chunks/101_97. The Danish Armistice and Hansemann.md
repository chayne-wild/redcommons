# 97. The Danish Armistice and Hansemann 



**[p. 382]**

THE DANISH ARMISTICE AND HANSEMANN

Cologne, August 10. We draw our readers' attention to our article on Denmark.3 The Danish newspapers have furnished us with some quite new disclosures about the behaviour of the "Government of Action" in the armistice affair.b So, in one way or another, Herr Hansemann's secret transgressions are coming to light after all.
