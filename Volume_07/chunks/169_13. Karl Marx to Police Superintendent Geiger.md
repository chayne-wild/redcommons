# 13. Karl Marx to Police Superintendent Geiger 



**[p. 561]**

KARL MARX TO POLICE SUPERINTENDENT GEIGER349

To Police Superintendent Geiger

Here.

Sir, I inform you that I have immediately appealed3 to the Ministry of the Interior against the paper drawn up by youb and that I continue to regard myself as before as a citizen of the German Empire.

Cologne, August 5, 1848

Editor-in-Chief of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung Dr. Karl Marx
