# 39. From the Minutes of the Committee Meeting of the Cologne Workers' Association on November 6, 1848. Entry Relating to Marx's Report on the Events in Vienna 



**[p. 598]**

FROM THE MINUTES OF THE COMMITTEE MEETING OF THE COLOGNE WORKERS' ASSOCIATION

ON NOVEMBER 6, 1848. ENTRY RELATING TO MARX'S REPORT

ON THE EVENTS IN VIENNA

 The President, Dr. Marx, gave a short report on the events in Vienna and stressed especially that it was only as a result of the manifold betrayal on the part of the bourgeoisie there that it became possible for Windischgrätz to capture the city

First published in the newspaper Freiheit, Printed according to the newspaper Brüderlichkeit, Arbeit No. 6, November 12, 1848 Published in English for the first time
