# 26. News from Switzerland 



**[p. 55]**

NEWS FROM SWITZERLAND

Berne, November 20. I have just heard from officials of the Fed- eral War Department that the German Central Authority is reported to have declared war on Switzerland.65 The courier arrived yesterday evening, and the Vorort was said to have gone into session at 11 o'clock the same evening. Measures had already been taken for serious war preparations. It was stated further that 50,000 imperial troops were concentrating on the Swiss frontier to begin hostilities.

I am passing this news to you exactly as I heard it. I myself do not believe it, although the source is reliable. I would not credit even the Imperial Government with such lunacy.
