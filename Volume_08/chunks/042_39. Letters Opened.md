# 39. Letters Opened 



**[p. 96]**

[LETTERS OPENED]

Cologne, November 28, 11 p.m. Two of the items of correspon- dence that have reached us this evening, one postmarked Berne and the other Paris, have clearly been opened by an official or semi-official hand. The seal was missing. The wafers with which the letters had been re-sealed were not yet dry. Sedlnitzky, too, is making propaganda with Windischgrätz.

[^1]: Written by Marx on November 28, 1848 Printed according to the newspaper

[^2]: First published in the Neue Rheinische Published in English for the first Zeitung No. 155 (special edition), time November 29, 1848
