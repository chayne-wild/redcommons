# 4. Decision of the Local Court Chamber 



**[p. 499]**

DECISION OF THE LOCAL COURT CHAMBER

Cologne, November 21. The Chamber of the provincial court of justice in Cologne this morning committed Dr. jur. Becker to the Court of Assizes. On the other hand, it decided with respect to the co-defendants Wächter, Bürgers, Engelsand associates that the records should be laid aside until these persons had given themselves up in custody.
