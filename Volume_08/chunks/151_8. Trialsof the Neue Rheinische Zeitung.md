# 8. Trialsof the Neue Rheinische Zeitung 



**[p. 504]**

TRIALS OF THE NEUE RHEINISCHE ZEITUNG

Cologne, December 5. A few days ago the rédacteur en chef of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung, Karl Marx, was once more summoned to the examining magistrate's court. Four articles induced the Central Authority to sue for libel: 1. Schnapphahnski; 2. an article from Breslau on Lichnowski; 3. an article in which there is talk about a "falsifying" report by a certain "comical Stedtmann"; 4. the publication of the "declaration of treason against the people", adopted in the Eiser Hall, against the Frankfurt majority on the Schleswig-Holstein question.386

The Neue Rheinische Zeitung is now most eagerly awaiting further libel charges from Berlin, Petersburg, Vienna, Brussels and Naples.
