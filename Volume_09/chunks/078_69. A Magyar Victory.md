# 69. A Magyar Victory 



**[p. 267]**

[A MAGYAR VICTORY]

Cologne, April 12. We are publishing a second edition today, not to inform our readers of yesterday's basically quite unimportant debates at Frankfurt but to report something much more important, namely that the Magyars have gained a significant victory over the imperial army, and that the imperial army, beaten all along the line, has withdrawn to below the walls of Pest. (See "Hungary".3)219
