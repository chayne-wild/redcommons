# 9. Decisions of the General Meeting of the Cologne Workers' Association Held on April 23, 1849 



**[p. 501]**

DECISIONS OF THE GENERAL MEETING OF THE COLOGNE WORKERS' ASSOCIATION

HELD ON APRIL 23, 1849

1. The general meeting will be held in future every Wednesday. 2. The provisional committee, consisting of Karl Marx, Karl Schapper, Wilhelm Wolff, Friedrich Anneke, Esser and Otto, elected by the Committee for the purpose of convening here in Cologne a congress of the Workers' Associations of the Rhineland and Westphalia, is confirmed.
