# 16. The Editors of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung 



**[p. 514]**

THE EDITORS OF THE NEUE RHEINISCHE ZEITUNG

Cologne, May 19. What has happened to the individual editors of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung is as follows: for his actions in Elberfeld Fr. Engels is being prosecuted like a criminal3; Marx, Dronke and Weerth, since they are not Prussian subjects, have to leave Prussia; F. Wolff and W. Wolff are being threatened with judicial proceed- ings, the former because he has not done military service, the latter because of political offences alleged to have been committed by him earlier in the old provinces. The Court has today refused to free Korff on bail.b

(Düsseldorfer Zeitung)

[^1]: Reprinted in the Deutsche Zeitung Printed according to the newspaper No. 140, May 22, 1849 and the Trier'sche text Zeitung No. 122, May 23, 1849 Published in English for the first time
