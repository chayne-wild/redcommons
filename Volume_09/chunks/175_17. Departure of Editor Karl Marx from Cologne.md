# 17. Departure of Editor Karl Marx from Cologne 



**[p. 515]**

DEPARTURE OF EDITOR KARL MARX FROM COLOGNE

Cologne. Yesterday morning Herr Karl Marx, the former editor-in- chief of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung, accompanied by some of the other editors, left Cologne and went to the region of the Upper Rhine; his activity there will be as successful as it has been here.426

First published in the Neue Kölnische Printed according to the newspaper
