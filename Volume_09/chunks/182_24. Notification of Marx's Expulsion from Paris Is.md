# 24. Notification of Marx's Expulsion from Paris Issued by the Commissioner of Police 



**[p. 526]**

NOTIFICATION OF MARX'S EXPULSION FROM PARIS ISSUED BY THE COMMISSIONER

OF POLICE433

City of Paris Year eighteen hundred and forty Quartier du Faubourg nine, nineteenth of July St. Germain

Office of the Prefect of Police3

We the undersigned Commissioner of Police of the Faubourg St. Germain,—

notify Mr. Marx (Charles), 29 years of age,b born at Treves (Prussia), writer, living at rue de Lille No. 55, of the decision of the Minister of the Interior,0 by which he is required to go to the department of Morbihan in order to reside there, and is obliged to comply with the measure adopted in respect of him.

Dourlens
