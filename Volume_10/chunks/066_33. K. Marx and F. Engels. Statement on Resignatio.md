# 33. K. Marx and F. Engels. Statement on Resignation from the German Workers' Educational Society in London 



**[p. 483]**

Karl Marx and Frederick Engels

[STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION FROM THE GERMAN WORKERS' EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY IN LONDON]343

To the Tuesday President of the Society in Great Windmill Street. Sir,

The undersigned herewith give notice of their resignation from the Society.

London, September 17, 1850

H. Bauer, K. Pfänder, J. G. Eccarius, S. Seiler, K. Marx, K. Schramm, F. Engels,

F. Wolff, W. Liebknecht,

Hain, Haupt, G. Klose
