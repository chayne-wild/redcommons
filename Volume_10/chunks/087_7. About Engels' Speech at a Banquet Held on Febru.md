# 7. About Engels' Speech at a Banquet Held on February 25, 1850, in Honour of the Second Anniversary of the February Revolution in France ( From a Newspaper Report) 



**[p. 607]**

ABOUT ENGELS' SPEECH AT A BANQUET HELD ON FEBRUARY 25, 1850,

IN HONOUR OF THE SECOND ANNIVERSARY

OF THE FEBRUARY REVOLUTION

IN FRANCE (From a Newspaper Report)430

Citizen Engels, editor of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung, made a speech in French which he ended, amidst thunderous applause, with a toast to the June insurgents.

First published in the Westdeutsche Zeitung Printed according to the newspaper No. 51, March 1, 1850
