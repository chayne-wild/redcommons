# Frederick Engels. Almeida 



**[p. 19]**

Frederick Engels

ALMEIDA19

A town of Portugal, in the province of Beira, between the rivers Coa and Duas Casa. Population, 6,200. It is strongly fortified, and was the scene of the defeat of the French, under Masséna, by the duke of Wellington, Aug. 5, 1811.
