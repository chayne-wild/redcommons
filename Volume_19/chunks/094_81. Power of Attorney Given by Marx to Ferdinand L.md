# 81. Power of Attorney Given by Marx to Ferdinand Lassalle for the Restoration of His Prussian Citizenship 



**[p. 359]**

[POWER OF ATTORNEY GIVEN BY MARX TO FERDINAND LASSALLE FOR THE RESTORATION

OF HIS PRUSSIAN CITIZENSHIP]1

I hereby empower Mr. Ferdinand Lassalle of Berlin, on my departure from that city, to vindicate my rights in the matter, now pending before the Royal Police Presidium, relating to the recognition of my status as a Prussian, restored to me under the Royal Amnesty of January 12 of this year or, alternatively, the possible granting of new naturalisation and permission to reside in Berlin. I further empower him to submit applications, to lodge petitions and appeals with the Royal Prussian Government as also with the German Federal Diet,346 and to avail himself of every right to which I am entitled, in the same measure to which I myself am entitled thereto.

Berlin, April 12, 1861

Dr Karl Marx
