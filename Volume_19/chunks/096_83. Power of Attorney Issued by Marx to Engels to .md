# 83. Power of Attorney Issued by Marx to Engels to Take Over Wilhelm Wolff's Estate 



**[p. 363]**

[POWER OF ATTORNEY ISSUED BY MARX TO ENGELS

TO TAKE OVER WILHELM WOLFF'S ESTATE]a

23 May, 1864

1, Modena Villas, Maitland Park, Haverstock Hill, London, N.W.

My dear Sir,

I hereby request you and give you full power to act as my representative at, and take all the necessary steps for, the execution of the will of our common friend, Wilhelm Wolff.

KARL MARX, Dr. Ph.

[^1]: First published in: Marx-Engels Gesamtaus- Printed according to the manu- gabe, Dritte Abteilung, Bd. 3, 1930 script

[^2]: a The whole manuscript is written in Marx's hand. The address on the envelope reads: "Fr. Engels, Esq. 6, Thorncliffe Grove, Oxford Street, Manchester."—Ed.
