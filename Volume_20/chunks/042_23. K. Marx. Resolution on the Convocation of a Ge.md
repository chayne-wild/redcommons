# 23. K. Marx. Resolution on the Convocation of a General Congress of the International in 1866 



**[p. 150]**

Karl Marx

[RESOLUTION ON THE CONVOCATION OF A GENERAL CONGRESS

OF THE INTERNATIONAL IN 1866]

The Standing Committee recommended to the Central Council to agree to the following as a recommendation to the conference 103:

The Central Council shall in 1866 convoke a general congress unless unforeseen circumstances shall necessitate its further postponement.

[^1]: Adopted by the Central Council on Reproduced from the Minute September 19, 1865 Book of the General Council

[^2]: First published, in Russian, in Generalny Sovet Pervogo Internatsionala. 1864-1866, Moscow, 1961
