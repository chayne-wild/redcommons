# 24. K. Marx. Resolution on the Procedure of Discussing the Programme of the Congress 



**[p. 151]**

Karl Marx

[RESOLUTION ON THE PROCEDURE OF DISCUSSING THE PROGRAMME OF THE CONGRESS104]

That the general purposes and ruling principles of the Association as laid down in the Address and Statutes be first defined before entering upon the discussion of the questions proposed by the conference.

Adopted by the Central Council on Reproduced from the Minute January 23, 1866 Book of the General Council

[^1]: First published, in Russian, in Generalny Sovet Pervogo Internatsionala. 1864-1866, Moscow, 1961
