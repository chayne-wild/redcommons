# 6. K. Marx. Draft Resolution on the Consequences of Using Machinery under Capitalism Proposed by the General Council to the Brussels Congress 



**[p. 9]**

Karl Marx

[DRAFT RESOLUTION ON THE CONSEQUENCES

OF USING MACHINERY UNDER CAPITALISM

PROPOSED BY THE GENERAL COUNCIL

TO THE BRUSSELS CONGRESS]8

Resolved: that on the one side machinery has proved a most powerful instrument of despotism and extortion in the hands of the capitalist class;

that on the other side the development of machinery creates the material conditions necessary for the superseding of the wages- system by a truly social system of production.

[^1]: Adopted by the General Council on Reproduced from the Minute August 11, 1868 Book of the General Council

[^2]: First published in French in a special supplement to Le Peuple Belge, No. 38, September 11, 1868
