# 15. K. Marx. Preamble to the Resolutions of the Geneva (1866) and Brussels (1868) Congresses of the International

Karl Marx

[PREAMBLE TO THE RESOLUTIONS OF THE GENEVA (1866) AND BRUSSELS (1868)

CONGRESSES OF THE INTERNATIONAL]48

As some of the resolutions passed at the first Congress may be considered as part of the platform of principles of the Internation- al Working Men's Association, and the reports of that Congress have had but a limited circulation, the General Council deems it advisable to republish them with the issue of the resolutions passed at the last Congress.
