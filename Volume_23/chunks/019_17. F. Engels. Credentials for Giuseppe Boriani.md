# 17. F. Engels. Credentials for Giuseppe Boriani 



**[p. 56]**

Frederick Engels

[CREDENTIALS FOR GIUSEPPE BORIANI]

November 30, 1871

Citizen Giuseppe Boriani is accepted member of the Interna- tional Working Men's Association and is authorised to admit new members and form new sections, on condition that he, and the members and sections newly admitted, recognise as obligatory the official documents of the Association, namely:

The General Rules and Administrative Regulations, The Inaugural Address, Resolutions of the Congresses, The resolutions of the London Conference of September 1871.

By order and in the name of the General Council

Secretary for Italy,
