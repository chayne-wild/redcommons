# 35. F. Engels. To the Saragossa Congress 



**[p. 139]**

Frederick Engels

[TO THE SARAGOSSA CONGRESS]100

London, April 6 [1872]

The General Council and the British Federal Council greet the Congress of Saragossa. Long live the emancipation of the proletariat!

Engels

[^1]: First published in La Emancipation, Printed according to the news- No. 44, April 13, 1872 paper

[^2]: Translated from the Spanish
