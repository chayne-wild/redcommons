# 61. K. Marx. Resolution on the Behaviour of Members of the General Council at the Congress 



**[p. 218]**

Karl Marx

[RESOLUTION ON THE BEHAVIOUR OF MEMBERS OF THE GENERAL COUNCIL

AT THE CONGRESS]I63

No member of the General Council should have the right to accuse another before the International Working Men's Congress until discussion of the election of members of the General Council.

Adopted at the sitting of the Sub- Printed according to the raanu- Committee on August 28, 1872 script

First published, in Russian, in Generalny Translated from the French Sovet Pervogo Internatsionala. 1871-1872, Moscow, 1965
