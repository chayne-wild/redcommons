VIII

RESIDENCE AND COMPOSITION OF THE NEXT GENERAL COUNCIL

1. Vote upon the change of residence of the General Council. Voted for the change, 26; against, 23; abstentions, 9.°

2. The seat of the General Council has been transferred to New York, by 30 votes against 14, for London, and 12 abstentions.0

"Abstained: Alerini, Dereure, Friedländer, Mac Donnell, Marselau, Morago, Farga Pellicer, Schwitzguébel."—Ed.
