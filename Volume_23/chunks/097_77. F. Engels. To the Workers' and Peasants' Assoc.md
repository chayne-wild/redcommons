# 77. F. Engels. To the Workers' and Peasants' Association of Lower Lombardy ( Section of the International) in Lodi 



**[p. 293]**

Frederick Engels

TO THE WORKERS' AND PEASANTS' ASSOCIATION

OF LOWER LOMBARDY (SECTION OF THE INTERNATIONAL) IN LODI216

London, November 13, 1872

Citizens,

It is with great pleasure that I have received the news of your formation as a section of the International, and I have immediate- ly informed the new General Council in New York. I append below the address for direct correspondence with the General Council, while I remain always at your disposal for any informa- tion, clarification or service you may desire.

Fraternal greetings,

Frederick Engels

[^1]: First published in La Plebe, No. 117, Printed according to the news- November 17, 1872 paper

[^2]: Translated from the Italian
