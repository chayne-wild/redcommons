# 18. K. Marx. To an Editorial Board in London 



**[p. 202]**

Karl Marx

[TO AN EDITORIAL BOARD IN LONDON]

[London,] December 19, 1877 41 Maitland Park Road, N. W.

Gentlemen,

Herewith a letter sent me from Breslau3 for forwarding to you. The sender, Horovitz, though not known to me, has written saying he is a member of the Breslau section of the Social-Democratic Party.

With kind regards,

Karl Marx
