# 48. F. Engels. To the Editor of the Freiheit 



**[p. 410]**

Frederick Engels

[TO THE EDITOR OF THE FREIHEIT]

[London,] July 22, 1881

To the Editor of the "Freiheit"

Mr. Norris A. Clowes, the New York Star's correspondent in Ireland, who has been recommended to me from America,465 has written as follows:

* "If Herr Most would like to make any statement to the New York Star public, I should be glad to give him the opportunity."*

To which I replied: * "If you wish to enter into communication with Mr. Most, you had better write to the Editor3 of the Freiheit, 252 Tottenham Court Road, W. London, who will be able to tell you whether such communication will be possible under present circumstances."*466

I hasten to bring this to your attention.

Yours faithfully,

F. E.
