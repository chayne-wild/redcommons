# 3. Frederick Engels' Notice on the Death of His Wife, Lydia Burns 



**[p. 567]**

[FREDERICK ENGELS' NOTICE ON THE DEATH OF HIS WIFE, LYDIA BURNS]

I herewith notify my friends in Germany that in the course of last night death deprived me of my wife Lydia, née Burns. London, September 12, 1878

Frederick Engels

First published in Vorwärts, No. 110, September 18, 1878

Printed according to the news- paper

[^1]: Published in English for the first time
