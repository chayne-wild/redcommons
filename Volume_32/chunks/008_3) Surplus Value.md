Apart from the confusion between LABOUR and labour capacity, Ricardo defines the AVERAGE WAGES or the VALUE OF LABOUR correctly. For he says that it is determined neither by the money nor by the means of subsistence which the labourer receives, but by the labour time which it costs to produce them, that is, by the quantity of labour objectified in the means of subsistence of the labourer.
