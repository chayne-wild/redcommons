MARX TO HEINRICH BÖRNSTEIN

Dear Sir,

It is impossible for me to let you have the review of Stirner before next week. Therefore deliver the specimen copy without my contribution; Bürgers will let you have an article in its stead.

Yours faithfully

Marx
