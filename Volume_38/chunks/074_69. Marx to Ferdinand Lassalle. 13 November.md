# 69. Marx to Ferdinand Lassalle. 13 November 



**[p. 69]**

MARX TO FERDINAND LASSALLE

IN DÜSSELDORF

[Cologne, 13 November 1848]

Dear Lassalle,

At your democratic-monarchist club237 you should resolve the following:

1. General refusal to pay taxes—to be advocated specially in rural areas;

2. Dispatch of volunteer corps to Berlin; 3. Cash remittance to the Democratic Central Committee in Berlin.238

For the Rhenish Democratic Provincial Committee239

K. Marx

(Private)

Dear Lassalle,

If you could send me some money, whether it be the 200 talers or the amount for the loan certificates, you would greatly oblige me. Send it to my wife, Cecilienstrasse 7. I have had a summons240

today and it is generally believed that I shall be arrested tomorrow.

Your

Marx
