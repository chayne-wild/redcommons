# 103. Marx to Eduar d von Müller- Tellering. 1 January 



**[p. 1850]**

103

MARX TO EDUARD von MÜLLER-TELLERING

IN LONDON

[Copy]

[London,] 1 January 1850

Dear Teilering,

Engels, Seiler, Weerth, Willich and I shall not be appearing at the meeting arranged for 3 January,301 for the following reasons amongst others:

1. The list of political refugees invited has been arbitrarily compiled. Thus, for example, C. Schramm and F. Wolff are not included.

2. Not one of the workers, who for years have been in the vanguard of German democracy in London, has been invited.

Your

K. Marx
