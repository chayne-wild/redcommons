MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London, about 20 August 1851]

Dear Engels,

Apropos, do for goodness' sake unite to Fischer in New Orleans. (Liebknecht is at present his regular correspondent). This is all the more important because it is precisely from New Orleans that the Kinkels, Ruges, etc., are hoping to draw subsidies. So don't forget to write to the man who complained in a letter to Liebknecht about your silence.

Your

K. M.
