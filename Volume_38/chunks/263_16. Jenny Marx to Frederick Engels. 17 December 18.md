JENNY MARX TO FREDERICK ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

London, 17 December 1851

Dear Mr Engels,

Moor has just asked me to send you in great haste a few words in reply to Weydemeyer's letter,0 just received. He will himself let you have an article on the French misère6 by Fridaye and wonders whether you might not be able to dispatch to America a humorous essay on the German nonsense, notably the hoaxing of Prussia by Austria, etc.562 I am also, on the orders of the powers that be, sending Freiligrath a reminder. We all look forward very much to seeing you here soon.526 Colonel Musch and the young ladies, his sisters,' send you their warm regards as does your

Jenny Marx
