# 53. Marx to Engels. 13 May 



**[p. 108]**

Few mortals, other than yourself, can boast of having received letters from me on 4 successive mail days; but I was anxious to show Papa Lupus which of us two is the more punctilious

First published in part in Russian in Voprosy istarii KPSS, No. 3, 1962 and in full in: Marx and Engels, Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 28, Moscow, 1962

Printed according to a letter from Cluss to Weydemeyer of 31 May 1852

Published in English for the first time

53

MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 13 May [1852] 28 Dean Street, Soho

Dear Engels,

Only a couple of lines today. Old Szerelmey, whose first volume is now ready, chivvies me daily about the advertisement for his book of battle reports.1" If you can't do it now, at least send me back his brouillon* by return.

Your

K. M.
