# 206. Marx to Engels. 15 February 



**[p. 413]**

If you can do something by Tuesday,a it would be very welcome, since I have to write to the Cape of Good Hope on the same day.b

Your

K. M.

First published abridged in Der Briefwech- Printed according to the original sei zwischen F. Engels und K. Marx, Bd. 2, Stuttgart, 1913 and in full in: Marx and Published in English for the first Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, time Vol. XXII, Moscow, 1929

206

MARX TO ENGELS

[London,] 15 February 1854 28 Dean St., Soho

DEAR Frederic, The lithographic sample enclosed herewith is all my own work.c

[^1]: IN MANCHESTER

[^2]: a 14 February. In compliance with Marx's request Engels sent him on 13 February the material which provided the basis for their joint article 'The War Question in Europe'. - b i.e. for the paper De Zuid-Afrikaan in Cape Town (see this volume, p. 405). - c See illustration facing p. 414.

[^3]: 15—2736
