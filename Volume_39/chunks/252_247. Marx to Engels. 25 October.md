MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 25 October 1854 28 Dean Street, Soho

Dear Frederic,

Enclosed a letter from Cluss. Schnauffer is dead. Pieper is a RESIDENT MASTER just outside London, at Eltham, Kent.

If you can let me have some military stuff for Friday,a so much the better. It is time to take a rational attitude to the Allies' conduct öf the war.

The Mademoiselle Bürgers business is very funny. As you already know, before she left Cologne she got herself a child, giving out that Lassalle was its father.

The post is leaving, hence

Vale*

Your

K. M.
