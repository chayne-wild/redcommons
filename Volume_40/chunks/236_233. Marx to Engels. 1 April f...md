MARX TO ENGELS396

[London,] 9 April 1859

Dear Frederick,

Pieper, finally cured and out of hospital (after a serious relapse), is off to Bremen. He has an ugly inflammation on his fore- head.

Have you been following the exposure of Palmerston over the Italian question (1848)?°

Anstey is back from Hong Kong and is threatening to have his revenge on Palmerston. As an opponent, the latter will find Anstey not a little dangerous—more so, at any rate, than Urquhart.398
