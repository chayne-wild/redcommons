MARX TO ENGELS

[London,] 20 September [1860] Dear Engels,

You must let me have the Eichhoffb and Lassalle's letter back by return of post.

I am counting on getting an article on Garibaldi, Lamoricière, or the CHINESE WAR for Saturday. '

Shall probably be writing to you at greater length tomorrow. In great haste. ,. 6 Your

K. M. •Although my finances here are in a horrible muddle, I have sent my wife and children to Hastings for a week. I have not the means to keep them there any longer. Unfortunately, they've had rain almost all the time.
