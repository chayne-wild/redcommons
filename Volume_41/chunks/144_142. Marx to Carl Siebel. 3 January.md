# 142. Marx to Carl Siebel. 3 January 



**[p. 1861]**

Next time, you might try and find time to write to me at somewhat greater length.

I have been very unfortunate of late. My wife was dangerously ill for 5 weeks and I had to lodge the 3 children elsewhere.

As regards Schily, we'll knock some sense into him again.

Salut.

Your

K. Marx

MARX T O CARL SIEBEL

IN BARMEN

[London,] 3 January 1861

Dear Siebel,

I have just noticed that a letter I sent you—in reply to the one received from you today—was wrongly addressed, namely, Elber- feld, poste restante, C. Siebel, jun. This I now rectify.

Salut.

Your

K. M.
