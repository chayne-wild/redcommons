# 249. Marx to Wilhelm Wolff. 4 October 



**[p. 417]**

You might perhaps write and tell me where and when you will be passing through London on your journey. If at all feasible, I shall come and meet you.

It is possible (even though all manner of things still stand in the way) that I shall enter an English railway office at the beginning of next year.3
