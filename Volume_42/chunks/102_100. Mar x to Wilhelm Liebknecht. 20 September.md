# 100. Mar x to Wilhelm Liebknecht. 20 September 



**[p. 99]**

MARX TO WILHELM LIEBKNECHT

IN LEIPZIG

London, 11 September 1865

Dear Liebknecht,

As soon as I know whether you are still in Hanover, I shall report to you the reasons for the interruption in correspondence, and indeed write at greater length.

On 25 September, a (PRIVATE) conference between the COUNCIL of the International here and the DELEGATES of the ADMINISTRATIONS in Switzerland, France, and Belgium will take place here. Can you not send yourself over here as DELEGATE from Germany?246

Salut.

Your

K. M.

MARX TO WILHELM LIEBKNECHT

[London,] 20 September 1865

I received yours yesterday afternoon, too late to post a letter here. Illness had much to do with my protracted silence. There were other reasons which I think useless now to dwell upon. Much business pressing upon my time just now, I can only return these few lines.
