# 195. Marx to Ludwig Kugelmann. 25 October 



**[p. 329]**

The London COUNCIL of the ENGLISH TRADE-UNIONS (its secretary is our President, Odger) is deliberating at the present moment as to whether it should declare itself to be the BRITISH SECTION OF THE INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION. If it does so, the control of the working class here will IN A CERTAIN SENSE pass into our hands, and we shall be able to give the movement a good 'PUSH ON'.18

Salut.

Your

K. Marx

195

^ . , [London,] 25 October 1866 Dear Friend,

These few lines immediately,

1. to thank you for your efforts; 2. to acknowledge receipt of your last letter and of the preceding ones;
