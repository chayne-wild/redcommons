# 22. Engels to Marx. 25 May 



**[p. 22]**

ENGELS TO MARX

IN LONDON

Manchester, 25 May 1868

Dear Moor,

You must come on Friday," because I have the afternoon free on Saturday and by then we shall be finished with everything in the house.

Enclosed V2 £20 and V2 £5 notes, the two other halves follow in another envelope. Till Friday then, let me know which train you are taking. You can travel from King's Cross by the new (Midland) line, which passes through the most beautiful part of Derbyshire. 9.10 from London 2.15 in Manchester 11.30 " " 5.45 " 3. " " 8.5 "

Your

F. E.
