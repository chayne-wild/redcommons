# 30. Engels to Marx. 25 June 



**[p. 29]**

MARX TO LUDWIG KUGELMANN29

IN HANOVER

London, 24 June 1868

Dear Friend,

All sorts of incidents have prevented me from writing to you. Now, too, only a few lines. My eldest and youngest daughters" both have SCARLATINA. I now recall that you spoke to me in Hanover" about a method of treatment as soon as the crisis has passed and the scaling process commences. Please be so kind as to explain this to me by return.

With best greetings to your dear wife and Frànzchen,

Yours

K. Marx

Liebknecht is growing increasingly dull under the impact of the South German stupidity. He is not enough of a dialectician to criticise both sides at once.b
