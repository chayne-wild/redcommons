MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 6 December 1868

DEAR FRED,

Please send me £3 by this Wednesday3 (the day after tomorrow), since the gas bill, etc., are due, and I have promised to pay by then.

Salut.

Your

K. M.
