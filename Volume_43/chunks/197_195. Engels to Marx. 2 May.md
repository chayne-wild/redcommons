# 195. Engels to Marx. 2 May 



**[p. 194]**

MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 1 May 1869

DEAR FRED,

Enclosed letter to our Belgian secretary3342 will become com- prehensible to you from the attached Cigale, the organ of the FRENCH BRANCH,72 one analogue of which has formed itself in Brussels b

and one (Comité de l'avenirc) in Geneva; ALL TOGETHER a few dozen men under Pyat's leadership.

My wife is still not well at all, but thinks she will be able to leave for Paris on Tuesday.

Once again it is just before Thomas, i.e., close of post. I think that tomorrow I shall at last be able to write more fully to you.

Salut!

Your

K. M.

195

ENGELS TO MARX

Manchester, 2 May 1869

Dear Moor,

I would have written long since if you had not left me waiting for the 'more tomorrow'.
