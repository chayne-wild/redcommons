# 204. Engels to Marx. 23 May 



**[p. 204]**

ENGELS TO MARX

IN LONDON

Manchester, 23 May 1869

Dear Moor,

Enclosed the promised first halves of 3 fivers, the other halves follow in a letter I shall put in the letter-box after close of post, which will thus arrive by the next post. Be so good as to let me know which train you will be coming on on Tuesday,353 so I can pick you up.

From Euston Square leaves 9 arrives 2 o'clock 10 " 3 12 " 5.35 " King's Cross Great Northern

10 " 3.10 12 " 6 St. Paneras,

Midland " 9 " 2.40 10 " 3

I hope you arrive at the latest by 3 pm, so we can still dine together. The weather has already changed and is nice and warm, and instead of sitting by the fire I am sitting with the window open for the first time today.

Best greetings to your wife and the girls.

Your

F. E.
