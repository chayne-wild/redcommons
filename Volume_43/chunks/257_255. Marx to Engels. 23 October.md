# 255. Marx to Engels. 23 October 



**[p. 254]**

ENGELS T O MARX

IN LONDON

Manchester, Friday, 22 October 1869

Dear Moor,

In all haste have enclosed the photographs, of which Jenny should select one. I have a bad grippe because of the great change in temperature, but it is starting to get better.

The book by Wakefield" is cited by Buttb as A View of Ireland and also An Account of Ireland, 2 volumes, 1812 or 1813.

Young's book is called: A Tour in Ireland, 2 volumes, date not given.

Ranc's novelc is very nice.

Best greetings.

Your

F. E.

255

MARX TO ENGELS

[London,] 23 October 1869

Best thanks for the photograms. I am also suffering from a bad grippe. I have already ordered the A. Young, and will write to the same man (Adams) about the Wakefield.d
