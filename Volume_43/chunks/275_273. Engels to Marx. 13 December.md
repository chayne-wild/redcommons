# 273. Engels to Marx. 13 December 



**[p. 272]**

MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 12 December 1869

DEAR FRED,

Just before the close of post I saw the letter from the Solingen people lying next to me; so I had forgotten to enclose it for you yesterday, which I do herewith/

Also a proof, sent me by Beesly, but the continuation of it is missing. The page with the portraits—Paris—belongs to Jen- nychen, so you must return it after PERUSAL.

Salut.

Your

Moor

Various resolutions will be taken about the Bee-Hive, d'abordb in order to register them in our Minutes Book, and then to publish them when there is an opportunity?

273

ENGELS TO MARX

Manchester, 13 December 1869 Dear Moor,

Enclosed—what I have discovered from Daviesd and otherwise about Irish land ownership ABOUT 1600. The post will be closing immediately, so I must keep things short today.
