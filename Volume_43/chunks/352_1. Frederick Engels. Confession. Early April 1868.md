# 1. Frederick Engels. Confession. Early April 1868 



**[p. 541]**

1

FREDERICK ENGELS669

CONFESSION

[London, early April 1868]

Your favourite virtue

in man quality in man in woman Chief characteristic Idea of happiness

" misery The vice you excuse

" detest Your aversion The characters you most dislike Favourite occupation

— Hero — Heroine — Poet

— Prose writer — Flower — Colour — Dish — Maxim — Motto

jollity to mind his own business not to mislay things knowing everything by halves Château Margaux 1848 to go to a dentist excess of any sort Cant affected stuck up woman Spurgeon chaffing and being chaffed none too many to name one Reineke de Vos,670 Shake- speare, Ariosto, etc. Goethe, Lessing, Dr Samelson Blue Bell any one not Aniline Cold: Salad, hot: Irish Stew not to have any take it aisy

F. Engels
