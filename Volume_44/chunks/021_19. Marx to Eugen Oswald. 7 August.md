# 19. Marx to Eugen Oswald. 7 August 



**[p. 37]**

Yours truly,

Karl Marx

First published, in the languages of the Printed according to the original original (English and French), in: G. Jaeckh, Die Internationale, Leipzig, Published in English in full for the 1904 first time

19

MARX TO EUGEN OSWALD

IN LONDON

London, 7 August 1870

Dear Oswald,

Iterum Crispinus.3

Frederick Engels gives you permission to add his name to the Address,15 but notabene on the express condition that you print the same reservation word-for-word as under my name.b

Yours,

K. M.

H. J. Rothschild, commerçant0 (a German, i.e. a Prussian), gives the same permission on the same condition.

Apropos. I take it that you did allow the passage to stand which hints at the defensive character of the war on the German side, albeit in an extremely diplomatic way?
