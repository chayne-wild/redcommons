# 29. Marx to Engels. 22 August 



**[p. 54]**

Is Kugelmann in Carlsbadb? I do not know where to send the portrait. 4

Best wishes from Lizzie and me to you all. I hope to hear good news from you soon about your rheumatism.

Your

F. E.

London, 22 August 1870

The onslaughts of the rheumatism were so severe that the family council resolved to send me to London to consult Dr Maddison. Hence went to London on Saturday0 afternoon from where I am returning to Ramsgate today.
