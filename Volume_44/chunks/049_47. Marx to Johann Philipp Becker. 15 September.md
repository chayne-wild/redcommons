# 47. Marx to Johann Philipp Becker. 15 September 

Yours,

K. Marx

First published in: Marx and Engels,

Works, Second Russian Edition, Vol. 33, Moscow, 1964

Printed according to the typewrit- ten copy

Published in English for the first time

47

MARX TO JOHANN PHILIPP BECKER

IN GENEVA

[London, 15 September 1870]

Dear Becker,

I sent a translationa to the Volksstaat a few days ago, because more urgent. However, this version is improved in a number of places.

Salut

Your

K. M.
