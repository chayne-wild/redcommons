MARX TO ADOLPHE HUBERT

[London,] 28 July 1871

Dear Citizen,

You would oblige me by coming to dine at my house next Sunday at 5 o'clock in the afternoon. You will find my friend from Brussels0 here and will be able to talk to him about the publication of the proceedings at the court martial.264

Fraternal greetings,

Karl Marx
