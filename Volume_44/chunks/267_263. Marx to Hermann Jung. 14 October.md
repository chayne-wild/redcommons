# 263. Marx to Hermann Jung. 14 October 



**[p. 439]**

Last Friday the wedding took place between. Jenny, my eldest daughter, and Longuet (who sends you his regards).*1

How goes it with public affairs in Spain? In my opinion you and many of the other proscribed Frenchmenc (save, perhaps, for those most compromised) will soon be able to return home.

I remain, dear Citizen,

Yours very sincerely,

Karl Marx

First published, in Russian, in Pravda, Printed according to the original 5 May 1970

Translated from the French

MARX TO HERMANN JUNG
