# 285. Engels to Maurice Lachâtre. After 14 February 

I shall try and unearth a copy of the Utopia (in English), but it may be difficult as all the old popular editions were bought up long since.

I must close here and regret that I am prevented by the need to catch the post from enclosing a few lines to your wife.d Please be kind enough to make my excuses. Does your family still live at 11 Braustrasse? We have only that address and the Volksstaat.

With best wishes from your

F. E.

[London, after 14 February 1873]
