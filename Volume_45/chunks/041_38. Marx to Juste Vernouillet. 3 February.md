I agree that it would be best to publish the last FASCICLES together, but that does not explain why Mr Lahure should have ceased printing three months ago. (He hasn't even sent me back the proofs of instalments 34 and 35.) I am hard-pressed by other work and am receiving letter after letter from my German,3 and likewise my Russian ,b publisher, urging me to complete the final editing of the second volume.62 So if, instead of printing and sending me the proofs as they come off the press, Mr Lahure continues to procrastinate, he alone will be to blame for any new delays and interruptions that may result. Not being desirous of writing further to that gentleman, I would request you to let him have your instructions.

Yours ever,

Karl Marx
