MARX TO PYOTR LAVROV

IN LONDON

[London,] 8 May 1875

My dear Friend,

I have just received a letter from a Berlin businessman* (a very upright man and of no political importance at all, at any rate in the eyes of the world and the police), who asks me to explain to him some points in Capital.90 When replying to him, which I did at once, I took the opportunity of requesting, in my turn, that he give me a safe address for your letters from Russia.91 My letter will go off today.

Yours ever,

Karl Marx
