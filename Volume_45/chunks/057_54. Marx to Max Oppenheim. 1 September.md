MARX TO MAX OPPENHEIM

IN PRAGUE

Karlsbad, 1 September 1875 Germania

Dear Friend,

By the time your letter of the 12th ult. arrived in London, I had already set off on my journey here.104 I found it extremely interesting. I shall be leaving here a week next Saturday (not this Saturday, that is, but the Saturday after)b and shall travel via Prague so that I may enjoy the pleasure of your company. However, urgent business will preclude my staying for more than 2 days.

With kindest regards.

Yours,

Karl Marx
