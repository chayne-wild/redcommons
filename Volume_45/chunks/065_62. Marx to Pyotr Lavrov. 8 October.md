# 62. Marx to Pyotr Lavrov. 8 October 



**[p. 61]**

MARX TO BERNHARD KRAUS

IN VIENNA

London, 30 September 1875

Dear Kraus,

May I most heartily commend to you my friend Leo Frankel, the bearer of these lines and a former member of the Paris Commune. I enclose another photograph, since the first was a poor one. I should be grateful if you could let me have your compendium of the new medical sciences." You would already have received the French edition of Capital, had not the publication of the last instalments been delayed and obstructed by the French police.17 It's bound to appear some day.

With best wishes from myself and daughter Eleanor,

Yours,

Karl Marx

62

[London,] 8 October 1875 41 Maitland Park Road, N. W.

Dear Friend,

I am sorry that I and my wife were not at home when you were kind enough to call.

I got the Quelques Mots d'un Groupe Russe etc. from Engelsb

and shall, with your permission, show it to another of my friends
