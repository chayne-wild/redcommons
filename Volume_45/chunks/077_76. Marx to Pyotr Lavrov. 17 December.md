# 76. Marx to Pyotr Lavrov. 17 December 



**[p. 76]**

MARX TO PYOTR LAVROV " 9

IN LONDON

[London, 17 December 1875]

Dear Friend,

Our good Leblanc is very ill and likes to be visited. Definite limits were set for the last sheets (i.e. the last instalment, 44th, of the French edition of Capital).17 Mr Lachâtre alleged that things had been so arranged with the printers that it would be impossible to go beyond the 44th sheet. This meant that I had to sacrifice the subject index which had already been drawn up. If I find a copy of it, I will send it to you.

Yours ever,

K. M.
