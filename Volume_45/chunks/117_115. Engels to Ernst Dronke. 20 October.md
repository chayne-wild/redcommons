ENGELS TO ERNST DRONKE

IN WATERLOO

[London,] 20 October 1876

To Dronke

My brother-in-law Blank writes to say that he absolutely and categorically refuses to enter into a transaction such as you suggest.203 Furthermore, he tells me he severed all connections with Hinsberg, Fischer 8c Co. three years ago, as is indeed the case.

I am sorry that nothing has come of the matter, since I should have liked to help you in so far as it was in my power to do so.
