MARX T O P H I L I P S T E P H E N KING

IN LONDON

[London,] 14 April 1877 41 Maitland Park Road, N. W. Dear Sir,

Please send me the Papers: The Greek United Church in Russia just laid before Parliament. Enclosed for account sent £1, 3, 3.

Yours truly,

Karl Marx
