MARX TO CARL HIRSCH

IN PARIS

[London,] 26 JUNE 1878

Dear Hirsch,

I cannot lay hands here on Bucher's reply in the Norddeutsche Allgemeine? The Frankfurter0 is said to have reprinted it.397

Kindly send it to me by return.

Yours very truly,

Karl Marx

Printed according to the original

Published in English in full for the first time
