ENGELS TO FRIEDRICH LESSNER

[London,] 12 September 1878 Half past one in the morning

Dear Lessner,

My poor wife" has just been released by death from her long sufferings.

I cannot send you the wine. However, you can have it fetched at any time.

Your

F. E.
