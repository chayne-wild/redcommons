MARX TO NIKOLAI DANIELSON

London, 19 September 1879 41 Maitland Park Road, Haverstock Hill, N. W.

My dear Sir,

I have just returned to London after almost two months rustication in the isle of Jersey494 and at other sea-side places.500 I was forced to do so and suspend all work during that time on medical advice because of nervous derangement. For this reason I was also unable to do justice to the mental food you were so kind as to forward me, but now I feel much reinvigorated and shall set at work with a will.

The book of Kowalewsky I got from himself.535 He is one of my 'scientific' friends who every year comes to London in order to explore the treasures of the British Museum.

[^1]: IN ST PETERSBURG
