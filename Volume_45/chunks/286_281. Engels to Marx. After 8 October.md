# 281. Engels to Marx. After 8 October 



**[p. 280]**

ENGELS TO JOHANN PHILIPP BECKER

IN GENEVA

London, 24 September 1879 Dear Old Man,

Your postcarda received. I have taken out a money order for £1 12s. which should come to 40.20 francs, so that you won't be in a hole if the cost of the power of attorney b amounts to a bit more. Marx is back,500 apparently in the very best of health, so no doubt work on the 2nd volume of Capital62 can now go briskly ahead.

As regards the progress of negotiations with our people in the land of the philistines, I shall let you have further news in due course.490

I n h a s t e- Your old friend,

F. E.
