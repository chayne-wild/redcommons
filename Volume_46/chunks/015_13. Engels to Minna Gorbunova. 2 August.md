ENGELS T O MINNA GORBUNOVA

London, 2 August 1880

122 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

Dear Madam,

In all haste — for several days my house has been full of friends, come on a visit from the provinces — herewith the address:

M. Da Costa, 40 rue Gay-Lussac, Paris. Since I have only seen the old gentleman once and very briefly, he will certainly not remember me. But all you need say by way of introduction is that Marx ob- tained the address expressly for you through his son-in-law Longuet — who is a friend of young Da Costa's. a
