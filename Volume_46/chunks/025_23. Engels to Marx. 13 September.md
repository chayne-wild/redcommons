# 23. Engels to Marx. 13 September 



**[p. 23]**

ENGELS TO MARX

IN LONDON

Bridlington Quay, 13 September 1880 7 Burlington Place

Dear Moor,

Have received the enclosed from Liebknecht. I am writing to tell him that, provided he hears nothing to the contrary within the next few days, we shall be back in London again from next Sunday, 19 September,30 and shall be expecting them (him and Bebel).60 So if you should be prevented in any way, kindly let me know.

I haven't had an answer to a postcard sent from here to your ad- dress in Ramsgate 38 on 29 August; no doubt it didn't arrive. Yester- day I heard from Lafargue, who said you would be returning to Lon- don today.

We shall be returning at the end of this week; wet weather since the day before yesterday, before which the weather was splendid.

I hope your wife is better. Warmest regards to all, likewise from Moore and Beust.

Your

F. E.
