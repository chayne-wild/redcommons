MARX T O FERDINAND FLECKLES

IN CARLSBAD

[London,] 12 November 1880

Dear Friend,

Herewith a card of introduction to my son-in-law Longuet who is to introduce you to Rochefort.

No doubt you will by now have received the letter my wife even- tually sent off; like all female invalids, she always despairs of the doc- tors who happen to be attending her.

With kindest regards,

Yours very sincerely,

Karl Marx

Dr. F. Fleckles, Carlsbad (Austria)
