MARX TO LAURA LAFARGUE

IN LONDON

Monte Carlo, 6 May 1882 Hôtel de Russie

My dear Cacadou,

Only a few hours since I arrived here at Monte Carlo.310 I even doubt whether I shall find the time enough to indite a letter an- nounced already to Engels (at all events he will but receive it a day later).

For the present I am obliged to run about on different errands. I enclose one photo for you, another for Fred"; no art can make the man look worse.

Old Nick
