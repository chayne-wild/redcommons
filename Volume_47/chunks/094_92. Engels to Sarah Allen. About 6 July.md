# 92. Engels to Sarah Allen. About 6 July 



**[p. 161]**

Nothing gives one more delight Than one's antagonists to bite, Than making scurvy jokes about This uncouth clod, that clumsy lout.a

Regards to Kautsky.

Your

F.E.

First published, in Russian, in Marx- Engels Archives, Book I, Moscow, 1924

Printed according to the original

Published in English in full for the first time

Yours, etc.

92

ENGELS T O SARAH ALLEN 2 6 2

IN LONDON

[London, about 6 July 1884]

Madam,

In reply to your note of the 5th arrived to hand yesterday and an answer to which was retarded by my absence from town, I beg to say that I consider Mr E. Aveling quite a desirable tenant and have no doubt that you will not regret having let your premises to him.
