# 188. Engels to Nikolai Danielson. 8 August 



**[p. 187]**

ENGELS TO EDUARD BERNSTEIN

IN ZURICH

[London, 24 July 1885]

Dear Ede,

Bebel has written to say he will be in Zurich on or about the 26th inst.— the enclosed lines are for hima; if he doesn't turn up, you will know how to deal with it.

Schorlemmer sends his regards. The coming autumn elections in France15 and England 2 " will be the beginning of the end, and I trust we shall also see the end of old William.0 Now that the Russians seem to have come to a standstill, we shall doubtless have to make a start ourselves. And, if the three great Western nations begin to move, that will also do.

Your

F. E.
