# 263. Engels to Karl Kautsky. 4 July 



**[p. 262]**

ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN LONDON

[Eastbourne,590] Friday, 2 July 1886

Many thanks for all the trouble you've taken. Please don't send me, apart from Nature, any English papers, or the Manchester paper which arrives on Saturdays, or, after Saturday, any more papers at all, apart from the Sozialdemokrat, or, after Monday, any more letters, for we shall be home again on Wednesdaya afternoon. The weather con- tinues magnificent — I await with interest the first election results from the larger towns 5 9 ' tomorrow morning. Best wishes from my family to yours.

Your

F.E.
