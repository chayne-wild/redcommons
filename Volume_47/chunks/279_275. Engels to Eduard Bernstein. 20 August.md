# 275. Engels to Eduard Bernstein. 20 August 



**[p. 473]**

On Tuesday,b Percy, Pumps and Lily went to pay a visit to the old Roshers at Walmer near Deal. They are supposed to return today, but I haven't heard anything. Yesterday was terribly close and hu- mid, but marvellous weather today. Nim is well and sends her kindest regards, as I do mine. I trust your wife is well.

Your

F.E.

Thanks for Sonnenschein; the parcel had been addressed to Regent's Park Road by mistake; it contained something I needed and for which I had already dunned them. 6 ' 4
