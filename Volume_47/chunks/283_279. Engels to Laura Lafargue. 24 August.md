# 279. Engels to Laura Lafargue. 24 August

Old Beckera will be arriving from Geneva on the 12th-13th to stay with me in London.

Mother Schack has announced her intention of coming to London in the middle of September along with the Wischnewetzkys.618

If Schorlemmer and the Lafargues turn up as well — postcard ar- rived 16th August from Schorlemmer in Bellaggio, Lake of Como — it will be pretty hectic.

ENGELS TO LAURA LAFARGUE

Eastbourne, 24 August 1886 4 Cavendish Place

My dear Laura,

This morning I receive your letter of the 20th from London with a perfect avalanche of other letters and papers—I cannot therefore do to-day more than send you the cheque £15. -

Old Beckera writes that he will be here (in London) with the van Kols' by 12th September.

[^1]: IN PARIS

[^2]: Johann Philipp Becker
