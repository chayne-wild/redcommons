ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

Eastbourne, 25 August 1886 4 Cavendish Place

Dear Baron,

Your letter sent to Offenbach will arrive in plenty of time, as Lieb- knecht doesn't leave Borsdorf until the evening of the 26th, i. e. will be in Offenbach on the 27th and won't leave Cologne until 1.40 p. m. on the 29th, assuming he is travelling via Flushing as he previously said he would.600

So I shall be arriving in London on Sunday eveningb between 9 and 10 5 " and hope to see you both that same night. I have asked
