# 306. Engels to Halliday Sparling. 7 December 



**[p. 305]**

ENGELS T O HERMANN SCHLÜTER 6 6'

IN HOTTINGEN-ZURICH

[London,] 7 December 1886

Please send me a copy of The Bakuninists at Work668; the only one I've got was in a bundle with other stuff. The thing is badly crumpled and requires close scrutiny. On Sundaya afternoon the soldierb

turned up here, where his wife had already been for several days—he's very satisfied with his success.600 Yesterday Paul, the Berliner,0 also turned up. Since I cannot easily apply myself to any really big jobs while they are here, I shall see if I can't get something or other ready for press for you; however, I would prefer to write my prefaces myself.

Kindest regards.

Yours,

F.E.

306

ENGELS T O HALLIDAY SPARLING

[London,] 7 December 1886 122 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

Dear Sir,

For your private information I beg to say that at present there is no English translation published of the work alluded to by you.669
