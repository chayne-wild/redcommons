42

ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN LONDON

London, 15 June 1887

Dear Kautsky,

O n what day did you write to Liebknecht about la Schack? I've just had a letter from Mrs Liebknecht from which it appears that she, at any rate, knows nothing at all about the matter. Would like to clear up the mystery.

Your
