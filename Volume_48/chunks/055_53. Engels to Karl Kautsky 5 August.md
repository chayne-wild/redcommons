# 53. Engels to Karl Kautsky 5 August 



**[p. 89]**

53

ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN VENTNOR

Eastbourne, 5 August 1887

A Dr Joseph Maria Baernreither, Austrian judge and member of the House of Deputies, actual author of a book on English benefit soci- eties147 and future ditto of a ditto on ditto Trades Unions, proposes to visit me. Can you tell me anything about this Bärenreuter3 or Bärenhäuter0} Many regards.

Your

Return Burger-Zeitungs herewith. When do you go home again?

First published in Aus der Frühzeit Printed according to the original des Marxismus. Engels Briefwechsel mit Kautsky, Prag, 1935 and in: Marx Published in English for the first time and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVII, Moscow, 1935

[^1]: F. E.

[^2]: a Literally, bear rider, the word Reuter having certain pejorative connotations, e.g. formerly also highwayman - b lazy-bones
