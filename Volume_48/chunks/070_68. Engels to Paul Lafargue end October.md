# 68. Engels to Paul Lafargue end October 



**[p. 67]**

ENGELS TO FRIEDRICH ADOLPH SORGE177

IN ROCHESTER

[London] 29 October 1887

Am sending you by today's post the Austrian calendar (Österreichischer Arbeiter-Kalender) with a biographical note,155 ditto Commonweal. Bebel and Bernstein are here in order, amongst other things, to make preparations for next year's international congress.178 Bebel most satisfied with the St Gall congress and likewise with the state of affairs in Germany.

Faced with spontaneous agitation on the part of the unemployed, both parliamentary groups179 in this country have shown how very much out of touch they are with the masses. Commonweal, as you will see, is completely at its wits' end.
