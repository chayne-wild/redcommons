ENGELS T O H E R M A N N SCHLÜTER

IN HOTTINGEN-ZURICH

[London], 23 January 1888

Dear Mr Schlüter,

You should have the Theory of Force by 20 February; you would be getting it even sooner but for the intervention of the English translation of the Manifesto* which I must polish off quickly with Sam Moore, the translator of Capital, who is here with me, otherwise I shall be missing a splendid opportunity.

a K. Marx and F. Engels, Manifesto of the Communist Party
