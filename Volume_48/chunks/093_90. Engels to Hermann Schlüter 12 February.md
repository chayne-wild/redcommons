# 90. Engels to Hermann Schlüter 12 February 



**[p. 147]**

In the meantime I am taking care of my eyesight which is improving under the treatment prescribed by my specialist, although he hasn't yet butchered my tear duct. But I have to spare my eyes.

My love to Laura.

Yours ever,

F. E.

First published, in the language of the Printed according to the original original (French), in: F. Engels, P. et L. Lafargue, Correspondance, t. II, Paris, Translated from the French 1956

90

ENGELS TO HERMANN SCHLÜTER

IN HOTTINGEN-ZURICH

London, 12 February 1888

Dear Mr Schlüter,

Unfortunately I shall not be able to send the ms.a I promised you by the 20th of this month, the reason being interruptions of all kinds, the arrival next week of the proofs of the Manifesto}' and the special care I am having to take of my eyes just now, while under treatment.

a F. Engels, The Role of Force in History - b The English edition of the Manifesto of the Communist Party by Marx and Engels
