ENGELS TO FRIEDRICH ADOLPH SORGE289

IN HOBOKEN

Niagara Falls, N. Y, 4 September 1888

Dear Sorge,

We have been here since Sunday0 morning and are enjoying ourselves greatly. The nature is beautiful here, the air is superb, the food is excel- lent and the nigger waiters are amusing—what more could one want, given this fine weather. As yet there have been no mosquitoes either, despite all the water. Our tour of the Oil Regions has been abandoned— whether we go to Chicago will be decided today—I think not. If we don't go we shall have adhered rigidly to your itinerary.
