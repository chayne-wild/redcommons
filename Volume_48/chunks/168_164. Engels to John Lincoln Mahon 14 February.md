164

ENGELS TO JOHN LINCOLN MAHON130

IN LONDON

[London], 14 February 1889

As far as I know G. J. Harney is still in England. Will let you know posi- tively as soon as I can, will try to write to him and ascertain his where- abouts at once. If I can be of any use to Mr. Atherley Jones,361 shall be glad to see him, am at home almost every evening.

Have not yet had the time to study your programme131 sufficiently to give an opinion. I am very much debarred from reading by gas by medical orders.

Yours truly

F. Engels

First published in: Marx and Engels, Reproduced from the original Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVIII, Moscow, 1940
