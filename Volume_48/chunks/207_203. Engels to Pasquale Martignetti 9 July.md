# 203. Engels to Pasquale Martignetti 9 July 



**[p. 350]**

Your article went off to Russia yesterday, REGISTERED. What you tell me about the wine-growers of Champagne471 is extremely interesting—the ruination of the peasant is now gathering speed, thanks to advanced capitalism!

It is most fortunate that Liebknecht should be staying with Vaillant; I have strong suspicions that he will make another attempt to join forces with the 'good elements' among the Possibilists, going 'over Brousse's head', as in March and April.

A kiss to Laura from myself and Nim.

Yours ever,

F. Engels
