# 251. Engels to Charles Rosher 19 January 



**[p. 250]**

ENGELS TO HERMANN SCHLÜTER591

IN NEW YORK

[London,] 15 January 1890

Burns has sent us word that he does not know the man concerned,3 so the firm is at any rate an obscure one.b

A great deal of flu around - so far we've been spared. Otherwise nothing new.

Your

F. E.

251

ENGELS TO CHARLES ROSHER

IN LONDON

[London, before 19 January 1890] [Draft]

Dear Charles,

Within two months of your engaging Percy you wrote to me asking for a loan, and in terms which hardly could leave any doubt of your intention to terminate that engagement unless I acceded to your wish.

[^1]: a George W. Reid - b See this volume, p.436
