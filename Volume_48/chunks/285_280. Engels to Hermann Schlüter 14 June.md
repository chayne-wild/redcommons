# 280. Engels to Hermann Schlüter 14 June 



**[p. 280]**

ENGELS TO HERMANN SCHLÜTER

IN NEW YORK

London, 14 June 1890

Dear Schlüter,

This is to advise you in much haste that you are welcome to reprint Marx's biography—but I have no time to finish it.666 You will find mate- rial in, inter alia, Marx's obituary in the Sozialdemokrat of March 1883.a

Congratulations on becoming 'chief. So far everything is going well over here, as also in Germany, where little Wïllieb is threatening to abolish universal suffrage—what better could befall us! In any case we're heading quite fast enough either for a world war or for a world revolution—or both.

Kind regards to your wife—I'm glad to hear she's in better health than she was over here.

Your

F. E.

First published in: Marx and Engels, Printed according to the original Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVIII, Moscow, 1940 Published in English for the first time

[^1]: a Frederick Engels, Karl Marx's Funeral - b William II
