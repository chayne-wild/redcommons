# 2. Engel s to Johann Heinrich Wilhelm Dietz. 5 August 



**[p. 4]**

ENGELS TO JOHANN HEINRICH WILHELM DIETZ

IN STUTTGART

London, 5 August 1890

Dear Mr Dietz,

Fischer has raised further objections to the immediate re-issue of the Origin* Aufondb this is quite acceptable to me, since I still have to go to the seaside where there can be no question of work, nor do I yet find work in any way conducive to my well-being. So I shall wait un- til the whole business has been settled to the satisfaction of all con- cerned.

Will you kindly take a look at the enclosed note for K. Kautskyc

before sending it on and, if necessary, do what is required.

Kindest regards.

Yours,

F. E.

[^1]: First published in: Marx and Engels, Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXVIII, Moscow, 1940

[^2]: Printed according to the original

[^3]: Published in English for the first time

[^4]: a F. Engels, The Origin of the Family, Private Property and the State. -h Lit.: at bottom- c See next letter.
