ENGELS TO PAUL LAFARGUE

AT LE PERREUX

London, 20 September 1890

My dear Lafargue,

Thank you for the information about Caron.37 Herewith my reply" to this amiable fellow who 'is in no doubt that my reply will be in the affirmative'. Don't talk to me of'LONDON ASSURANCE' or YANKEE jour- nalists' cheek. The Germans and French outdo them by far and, what's more, with an elegant effrontery that suits them to a T. However I'm not sure that it isn't my own dear countrymen who don't carry off first prize.

Nothing new to report here.
