76

ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN STUTTGART

London, 23 February 1891

Confidential

Dear Kautsky,

Seeing that you have sent me Bebel's letter and that one good turn deserves another, I have worded the enclosed letterb in such a way that you can send it to Bebel should you also consider it desirable in the interests of peace. This I leave entirely to you.

Your notes on the Vorwärts article are very good.171 Likewise your proposal to remind Bebel of the indifference with which Schramm's attacks on Marx were allowed to pass.

In great haste—the post goes in 5 minutes.

Your

F.E.
