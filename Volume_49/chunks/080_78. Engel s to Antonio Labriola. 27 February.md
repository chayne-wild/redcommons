First published, in Russian, in Bolshevik, Printed according to the original No. 22, Moscow, 1931

78

ENGELS TO ANTONIO LABRIOLA 1 7 6

IN ROME

[Copy]

London, 27 February 1891

 Unfortunately I cannot place the old ms. on Stirner at Mr Mac- kay's disposal. If it is published it will be by me or my assigns. But to hand over to a third party for such use as he may think fit an unpub- lished ms. in which Marx had a hand is something I have no right to do, nor would I do so if I had. I have had some highly peculiar expe- riences in this respect. Never again shall I part with unica,h whatever the circumstances. And besides, the ms. is a tome which in print would be as thick as Stirner's Einziger^ itself—it's very tattered and fragmentary — and is still in need of rearrangement

(Frederick Engels)
