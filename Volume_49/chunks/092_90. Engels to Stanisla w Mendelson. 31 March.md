ENGELS TO STANISLAW MENDELSON

[London,] 31 March 1891

Dear Citizen Mendelson,

I have just received the letter a copy of which is enclosed here- with. 202 I have absolutely no knowledge of how things stand between you and Wrôblewski. It is a matter that does not concern me in the slightest.a But in view of the delicate situation in which this letter has placed me, I have no choice — and I think you will agree with me
