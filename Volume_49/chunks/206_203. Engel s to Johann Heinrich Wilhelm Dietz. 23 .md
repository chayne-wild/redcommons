# 203. Engel s to Johann Heinrich Wilhelm Dietz. 23 April 



**[p. 203]**

ENGELS TO JOHANN HEINRICH WILHELM DIETZ

IN STUTTGART

London, 23 April 1892 122 Regent's Park Road, N.W.

Dear Mr Dietz,

I beg to acknowledge your esteemed favour of the 20th inst. I also agree that you should, with Mr Otto Wigand's consent, arrange for the publication of the Condition of the Working-Class in England in your Internationale Bibliothek469 on terms which remain to be set- tled in detail- between ourselves.

I therefore look forward to seeing your proposals on the same and remain

Yours very sincerely,

F. Engels

[^1]: First published in: Marx and Engels, Printed according to the original Works, First Russian Edition, Vol. XXIX, Moscow, 1946 Published in English for the first time
