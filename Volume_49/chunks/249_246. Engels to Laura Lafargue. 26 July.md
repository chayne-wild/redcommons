# 246. Engels to Laura Lafargue. 26 July 



**[p. 486]**

I hear from Gumpert that you have written and told him your mother is unwell and has taken to her bed. I hope for the best but she is very old and Carl's death will have hit her very very hard. But I hope that despite everything you will be able to send me better news before long.

Yours very sincerely,

F. Engels

ENGELS TO LAURA LAFARGUE

London, 26 July 1892

My dear Laura,

I receive this minute, 9.45 evening—Sonnenschein's account which results in £5.9.2., 3/5-ths of which go to Mohr's heirs, that is £ 1.1.10 each, of which cheque enclosed.

I hear from Tussy that you are very much out of sorts just now, hope you will mend soon!

[^1]: AL LE PERREUX
