ENGELS TO FILIPPO TURATI

IN MILAN

[London, 1 February 1893] Dear citizen Turati,

Here is the preface.b You are going too quickly for me in Italy. Your Panamino, 106 which threatens to become a Panamone, is undergoing evolutions and vicissitudes at such a rate that we here in London cannot keep up with events in Rome.
