ENGELS TO HENRY DEMAREST LLOYD

IN CHICAGO

London, 27 May 1893 122 Regent’s Park Road, N. W. Dear Sir, I beg to acknowledge receipt of your book A Strike of Millionaires against Miners, 2nd ed., for which please accept my best thanks. 211 I shall read it with great interest.
