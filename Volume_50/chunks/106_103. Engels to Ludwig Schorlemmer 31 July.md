# 103. Engels to Ludwig Schorlemmer 31 July 



**[p. 102]**

ENGELS TO NICOLAS PETERSEN

IN COPENHAGEN

London, 31 July 1893 122 Regent’s Park Road, N. W. Dear Mr Petersen, I am prevented from answering your letter of the 3rd inst. in detail since I will be leaving for the Continent tomorrow. 189 After my return I will try and go into the questions you raise. Meanwhile thank you for sending those very interesting statistics. Warmest greetings,

Your

F. Engels

Printed according to the original Published in English

for the first time

103

ENGELS TO LUDWIG SCHORLEMMER

IN DARMSTADT

London, 31 July 1893 My dear Schorlemmer,

Unfortunately there will not be time to see you in Darmstadt, as I shall be traveling straight through to Switzerland and Austria.

Nothing definite from Siebold. He too has been sent to Switzerland for the Alpine climate and won’t be back for some time. Nothing can be done about the matter until I return. 199

First published, in the language of the original (German), in: Meddelelser om Forskning i Arbejderbevaegelsens Historie, No. 11, Copenhagen, October 1973

[^1]: I do not wish to set off on my trip to the Continent, 189 which begins tomorrow, without replying to your letter of the 27th.
