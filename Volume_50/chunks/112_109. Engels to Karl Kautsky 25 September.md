# 109. Engels to Karl Kautsky 25 September 



**[p. 188]**

When we came to Prague, there was the little état de siege0 in force there. Nobody in our hotel ever thought of asking for our names! Voilà ce que c’est que l’Autriche: Despotismus gemildert durch Schlamperei.c

Amitiés àd Paul. Ever yours,

F. Engels

Louise, Bebel und Frau grüssen euch beide herzlichst.e Your copy of Paul’s article and Paul’s letter we gave to Adler who used them for his very good article in the Arbeiter Zeitung. 259
