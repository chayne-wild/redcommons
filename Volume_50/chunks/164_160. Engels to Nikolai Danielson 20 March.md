ENGELS TO NIKOLAI DANIELSON

IN ST. PETERSBURG

London, 20 March 1894 122 Regent’s Park Road, N. W.

My dear Sir, Today I forwarded to you, registered, sheets No. 1-6 (up to page 96) of Vol. II P containing the greater part of Section 1. The continuation will follow as it reaches me.

Yours very faithfully
