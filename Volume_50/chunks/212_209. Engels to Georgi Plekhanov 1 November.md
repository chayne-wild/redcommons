# 209. Engels to Georgi Plekhanov 1 November 



**[p. 208]**

ENGELS TO MARIA MENDELSON

IN LONDON

London, 26 October 1894 41 Regent’s Park Road, N. W.

Dear Madame Mendelson, At last we are able to receive our friends—even though the house still bears as it were the marks of seige and bombardment—we will have the pleasure of seeing both of you on Sunday evening.a Louise and the doctorb send their greetings,

Yours sincerely,

F. Engels

Published in English for the first time
