# 231. Engels to Pyotr Lavrov 18 December 



**[p. 388]**

Post time. Must close. I owe you for your share of the

Sonnenschein account for Capital (English) £1.31.- 1/3 Share of £5.—received from Neue Zeit for the 2 chapters

from Vol. II Ic £1.13.4 And allow me to add as a remembrance that

Christmas is coming £ 5. -. - Covered by cheque herewith £7.16.5. Puddings could not be made this year, the little girl of Louise’s (which is prosperous and gaining nearly a pound a week in weight) has stopped that. But Paul will have his cake.

Ever yours

F. Engels
