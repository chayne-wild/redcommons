ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN STUTTGART

[London,] 12 January 1895 41 Regent’s Park Road N. W.

Dear Baron,

Just now I received a registered letter from Mr Stiebeling containing a curious reply to my prefaceb and the even more curious request that I should arrange for it to be published in the Neue Zeit. 475 All I can tell the man is that the columns of the Neue Zeit are not at my disposal, but that I should be very glad if the editors were to disseminate this, his reply, as widely as possible. And with that I bid Mr Stiebeling a fond farewell and leave him to his fate.

[^1]: a K. Marx, The Crisis and the Counter-revolution. — b to the third volume of Capital
