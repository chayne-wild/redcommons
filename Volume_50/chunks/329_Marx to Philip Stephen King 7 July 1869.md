# Marx to Philip Stephen King 7 July 1869 



**[p. 5]**

MARX TO PHILIP STEPHEN KING 628

IN LONDON

London, 7 July 1869

Dear Sir,

Please send me 1) The Report on Emigration, just issued, 2) ditto: On Water Supply

Yours truly

K. Marx
